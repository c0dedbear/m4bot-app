M4Bot

Audiobook Converter for Mac

Convert MP3, WAV, and M4A files to M4B format with automatic chapter creation and metadata management. All processing happens locally on your Mac. Optimized for Apple Silicon for maximum speed.

Download on the Mac App Store

Key Features

Convert Audio Formats

Convert MP3 to M4B, WAV to M4B, and M4A to M4B. Merge multiple audio files into a single audiobook with high-quality AAC encoding.

Automatic Chapters

Chapters are automatically created from your audio files. Edit chapter titles, reorder files, and customize metadata before conversion.

Metadata Management

Add book information including title, author, narrator, year, and cover art. Metadata is automatically extracted from source files.

Audio Quality Control

Customize bitrate (64-320 kbps), sample rate (44.1/48 kHz), and channel layout (mono/stereo) to match your preferences.

100% Local Processing

All conversion happens on your Mac. Your files never leave your computer. No cloud uploads, no data collection, complete privacy.

Apple Books Compatible

Created M4B files work perfectly with Apple Books, iTunes, and all major audiobook players with full chapter navigation support.

Apple Silicon Optimized

M4Bot is designed exclusively for Macs with Apple Silicon processors, ensuring high-speed conversion and optimal performance for all your audiobook processing needs.
